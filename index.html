About Me

I'm an American English language educator currently based in Suzhou, Jiangsu, China, where I live with my wife and two children. With over 16 years of teaching experience in China, I've had the privilege of working with students from kindergarten through postgraduate levels, specializing in English for Academic Purposes (EAP) and English for Science.

I currently serve as the Head of the English Language Center at Soochow University's College of Nano Science & Technology, where I lead curriculum development, manage a team of EAP lecturers, and work closely with science professors to create meaningful English learning experiences for students in STEM fields.

Aside from teaching, I am interested in statistics, data science, and machine learning, exploring how these fields can enhance educational practices and open new possibilities.
